Credit Points & Wallet

The Credit Points & Wallet feature drives repeat visits, advance payments, and client loyalty without impacting profits. With automated points, customizable rules, WhatsApp updates, and seamless integration, salons enjoy quicker checkouts, improved retention, and steady revenue.

Instant Appointment Scheduling

Online appointments enable 24/7 booking with real-time availability, smart staff allocation, and instant WhatsApp confirmations. Integrated with memberships, packages, wallet payments, and multi-branch control, it optimizes operations, boosts bookings, and enhances client satisfaction.

Smart Payments

Smart Billing simplifies invoicing with automatic GST/VAT, multiple payment options, and seamless integration with memberships, packages, and wallets. It offers WhatsApp invoices, staff commissions, discount controls, and real-time reports for efficient, transparent, and professional checkouts.

Customer Reviews & Ratings

The Customer Reviews & Ratings feature automates WhatsApp feedback collection, tracks staff performance, and provides real-time alerts and multi-branch analytics. Inquiry Handling Workflow

Easy Salon Software’s Inquiry Management captures, tracks, and follows up on leads with instant staff alerts, WhatsApp responses, and smart reminders. It boosts lead conversion, enhances accountability, and helps salon owners turn inquiries into paying customers across multiple branches.

Results-Driven Marketing

Results-Driven Marketing empowers salon owners to run targeted, automated campaigns via WhatsApp, text, and email. With smart segmentation, automatic triggers, templates, and real-time tracking, salons boost repeat visits, fill slots, and drive steady revenue without relying on agencies.

Client Retention Strategy

Client retention strategy helps reduce drop-offs and build loyalty with data-driven reminders, personalized follow-ups, and reactivation workflows. Integrated with loyalty points, memberships, and real-time analytics, it converts one-time visitors into repeat clients, boosting revenue and growth.
