All-in-one salon management for automating bookings, billing, staff, inventory, and customer engagement, powered by WhatsApp and built for modern, multi-branch salons.

Appointment

Appointment Management enables 24/7 bookings via WhatsApp, online, or in-store. Real-time sync, automated reminders, and smart staff allocation reduce no-shows, improve satisfaction, and streamline operations, while integrations with billing and CRM boost efficiency and revenue.

Instant Check-In

Instant Check-In lets customers check in via QR codes or links, eliminating queues and reducing front-desk workload. With live updates, tamper-proof attendance tracking, and seamless integration with appointments and billing, elevate efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Browse Our Offers & Gallery

The Showcase feature lets businesses display services, promotions, and brand highlights with images or videos. Easily update and share via WhatsApp or booking links to boost upselling, brand trust, and consistent promotions across branches, driving conversions.

Client Feedback Hub

Client Feedback Hub automates feedback collection via WhatsApp, text, or email, helping businesses build trust and attract new clients. Centralized tracking enhances visibility, identifies the strengths and gaps, and protects reputation, driving growth and loyalty.
